Tribute to A.P.J Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J Abdul Kalam

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (1931-2015) was an iconic Indian aerospace scientist, statesman, and author, known as the "Missile Man of India" for his pivotal role in developing India's missile and nuclear programs, and as the beloved "People's President" (2002-2007) for his humility and dedication to inspiring youth, rising from humble beginnings in Rameswaram to serve his nation with vision and simplicity.

Achievements
  • Developed Indian's first satelite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Led Indian's successful nuclear test in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
